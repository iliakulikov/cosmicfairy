We are a new-wave label focused on melodic and deep techno — where emotion meets technology.

Founded by artist Sasha Miro, our mission is to connect body and soul through sound, light, and motion.

Based in New York, inspired by Berlin, made for the universe.

We believe electronic music is more than sound — it’s energy, storytelling, and emotional connection.

Our releases blend cinematic atmosphere, deep rhythms, and human emotion.

We aim to build a global collective of artists redefining melodic techno.
